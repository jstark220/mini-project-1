About Me

Driven by curiosity, grounded in accounting.

I am a junior at Babson College studying accounting and finance, and I will be joining PwC as an Audit Intern. My interest in accounting goes beyond the numbers — I am drawn to what financial reporting reveals about how organizations create and sustain value, and how capital markets process that information. As part of the Babson College Fund, I actively manage a portion of Babson's endowment through equity research and stock trading on the tech team.

At Babson, I have had the opportunity to work alongside both students and faculty who share a genuine passion for business. The entrepreneurial culture there has shaped the way I think: I approach every problem — whether in an audit engagement or in everyday life — with an eye toward understanding underlying systems and asking the right questions.

Outside of coursework, I write about accounting, auditing, and capital markets on my X blog, Materiality Check. My goal is to make technical accounting topics approachable for a broader audience — from fellow students to finance professionals to curious readers.
